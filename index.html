Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bi Bible (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users. The app does not require account creation and does not request personal details such as name, email, phone number, or location.

2. Device and App Data

We may store limited data locally on your device to improve functionality:

  • Downloaded Bible content for offline reading
  • App preferences (selected Bible version, theme settings)

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. Bible Content and Downloads

The app downloads Bible text (including ZIP files) from external sources to provide access to different Bible versions.

  • Used only within the app
  • Stored in app-private storage
  • Not linked to personal identity
  • No tracking of individual downloads

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If discovered, we will delete it immediately.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to ensure locally stored data remains protected within the app environment.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected in the app or store listing.
